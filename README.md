
## **AI & Life Skills Training Program with PBL and Cybersecurity Workshop**

---

## **Program Overview**

This training program is a **5-day structured batch plan** designed to build **AI awareness, life skills, communication skills, project-based learning (PBL) capability, and cybersecurity awareness** among students.
The program integrates **technical knowledge**, **soft skills**, **hands-on activities**, and **certification guidance** using industry-recognized platforms.

---

## **Program Duration**

**5 Days** (3–5 hours per day)

---

## **Target Audience**

* Students
* Youth participants
* Entry-level learners interested in AI & professional skills

---

## **Platforms & Tools Used**

* IBM SkillsBuild
* Credly
* ChatGPT (GPT AI)
* Canva
* Gemini
* Google Forms / Magic Bus Forms

---

## **Day-wise Training Plan**

---

### **Day 1: Orientation, Registration & Introduction to AI**

**Objectives**

* Complete student onboarding
* Introduce AI fundamentals
* Familiarize students with platforms and documentation

**Sessions & Activities**

* Orientation & batch introduction
* Registration on **IBM SkillsBuild**
* Registration on **Credly**
* Completion of **first IBM SkillsBuild module**
* Demo of **Magic Bus form filling process**
* Introduction to Artificial Intelligence

  * What is AI
  * Real-life applications
  * AI in education and careers

**Outcome**

* Successful registrations
* First AI module completed
* Students comfortable with platforms and forms

---

### **Day 2: Core AI Concepts**

**Objectives**

* Understand AI sub-domains
* Learn ethical AI usage
* Hands-on exposure to one AI tool

**Sessions**

* Natural Language Processing (NLP)
* Machine Learning (ML)
* Deep Learning (DL)
* AI Ethics (bias, privacy, responsibility)
* One AI tool demonstration and practice

**Outcome**

* Conceptual clarity of AI
* Awareness of ethical considerations
* Basic AI tool usage skills

---

### **Day 3: Project-Based Learning (PBL) & CSR Communication Skills**

**Objectives**

* Introduce PBL methodology
* Begin project work
* Build communication skills

**Sessions**

* Introduction to Project-Based Learning (PBL)
* Group formation and problem identification
* PBL documentation guide

  * Problem statement
  * Objectives
  * Solution approach
* CSR Communication Skills – Module 1

**Outcome**

* PBL projects initiated
* Students understand documentation structure
* Improved communication awareness

---

### **Day 4: CSR Skills, Certification & Credit Points**

**Objectives**

* Complete CSR modules
* Guide students on certification process

**Sessions**

* CSR Communication Skills – remaining modules
* Credit points explanation
* Checking credit points on platforms
* Step-by-step guide to download certificates
* Query resolution session

**Outcome**

* CSR modules completed
* Students independently able to track credits and certificates

---

### **Day 5: Cybersecurity Workshop & Program Closure**

**Objectives**

* Create cybersecurity awareness
* Complete program closure activities

**Sessions**

* Cybersecurity workshop

  * What is cybersecurity
  * Types of cyber threats
  * Spam, phishing & attacks
  * Online safety precautions
* Poster making activity using:

  * Canva
  * Gemini
  * ChatGPT (GPT AI)
* Certificate collection
* Feedback video recording
* Social media post creation
* Final group photo with standee or poster

**Outcome**

* Cybersecurity awareness
* Creative outputs (posters, videos)
* Successful program completion

## **Energizer Activities (Used Between Sessions)**

---

### **1. 7C of Communication Activity**

**Objective**
Understand effective communication principles.

**7C**
Clear, Concise, Concrete, Correct, Coherent, Complete, Courteous

**Method**

* Divide students into 7 groups
* Assign one “C” to each group
* Groups present good and poor communication examples

**Learning Outcome**

* Improved verbal and written communication

---

### **2. Clap Activity – Understanding Capability**

**Objective**
Build confidence and self-awareness.

**Method**

* Students clap softly, medium, loud
* Clap at full capability and half capability

**Learning Outcome**

* Understanding personal potential and confidence

---

### **3. Time Management Analysis Activity**

**Objective**
Analyze daily time usage.

**Method**

* Students list daily activities
* Categorize as productive, unproductive, necessary
* Identify time wasters

**Learning Outcome**

* Better planning and prioritization

---

### **4. Chinese Whispering Activity**

**Objective**
Understand communication gaps.

**Method**

* Message passed student-to-student
* Compare original vs final message

**Learning Outcome**

* Importance of clarity and listening skills

---

### **5. Urgency Management Activity (4D Method)**

**Categories**

* **DO** – Important & urgent
* **DECIDE** – Important, not urgent
* **DELEGATE** – Urgent, not important
* **DELETE** – Not urgent, not important

**Objective**
Teach decision-making and task prioritization.

**Learning Outcome**

* Improved productivity
* Reduced stress

---
